Li Sun

Assistant Professor

North China Electric Power Univ ccesunli@ncepu.edu.cn

Biography

Dr. Sun, Li is currently an assistant professor at North China Electric Power University, Beijing. He received the Ph.D. Degree in Computer Science from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2021 supervised by Prof. Su, Sen, and visited University of Illinois at Chicago advised by Prof. Philip. S. Yu (ACM/IEEE Fellow).

His research areas are machine learning and data mining with special attentions to graphs. He has published in WWW, AAAI, IJCAI, ICDM, CIKM and IEEE TKDE, ACM TWEB, ACM TIST. He has been invited as program committee in KDD, WSDM, AAAI, IJCAI, ECAI and conference committee in IEEE BigData, IEEE SocialCom, and served as reviewer in ACM TKDD, ACM TWEB, ACM TIST and IJMLC.
